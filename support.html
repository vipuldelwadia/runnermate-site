Frequently Asked Questions

Why does RunnerMate need access to my Health data?

RunnerMate reads your step count, running distance, and exercise history to set a realistic starting level for your 5K plan. Without this data, it can't personalize your programme.

Does RunnerMate require an Apple Watch?

No — RunnerMate works on iPhone alone. An Apple Watch adds real-time guidance during runs via WorkoutKit.

Does the AI advisor require a specific iPhone model?

The AI running advisor requires Apple Intelligence (iPhone 15 Pro or later with iOS 26). On older devices, RunnerMate provides advice using built-in guidance instead.

What is the minimum iOS version?

RunnerMate requires iOS 26 or later.
